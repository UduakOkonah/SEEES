History of SEEES

The department of Electrical/Electronics stared in the days of college of technology, Calabar in 1973. When the college name was changed to the polytechnic Calabar, the department continued, until September 2002, when the administration of Mr. DONALD Duke merged four institutions to become Cross River University of Technoogy Calabar.
